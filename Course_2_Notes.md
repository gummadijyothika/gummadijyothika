## ðŸ’¡ The SMART Framework
* **S**pecific: Does the question address a specific problem?
* **M**easurable: Can the answer be tracked with numbers?
* **A**ction-oriented: Will the answer lead to a change?
* **R**elevant: Does this solve a real business problem?
* **T**ime-bound: Is there a specific timeframe?

### Practice Example:
**Vague Question:** "How do we get more customers?"
**SMART Question:** "Which marketing channel (Email vs. Social Media) resulted in the highest conversion rate for new users in Q1 2026?"
